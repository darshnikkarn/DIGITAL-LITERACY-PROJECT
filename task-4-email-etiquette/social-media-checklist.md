Social Media Do's and Don'ts Checklist


The Do's:

1.Do review your privacy settings regularly: Ensure you know exactly who can see your posts and personal details.

2.Do verify information before sharing: Check the credibility of news or sensational posts to avoid spreading misinformation.

3.Do maintain a professional tone: Assume potential employers or professors might see your public comments.

4.Do curate your digital footprint: Highlight academic projects, coding challenges, or extracurricular achievements.

5.Do respect diverse opinions: Engage in healthy, constructive discussions rather than resorting to hostility.


The Don'ts:

1.Don't overshare sensitive data: Never publicly post your exact location, phone number, or travel itineraries in real-time.

2.Don't engage in cyberbullying: Avoid participating in online harassment, dog-piling, or spreading rumors.

3.Don't click on unverified links: Be wary of direct messages containing strange links, even if they appear to come from a friend.

4.Don't post impulsive emotional rants: Step away from the keyboard when angry; the internet is permanent.

5.Don't use inappropriate language or media: Avoid posting content that violates platform guidelines or could damage your reputation.
